About Misk Beirut

SINCE 1982

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Beirut during a time of both challenge and vibrancy, Misk began as a humble kitchen dedicated to the forgotten flavors of the Levant. Our journey started with a single wood-fire oven and a commitment to hospitality that treats every guest as kin.

Our philosophy is rooted in the Mediterranean spirit of abundance and simplicity. We believe that the best meals are those where the ingredients speak for themselves, sourced from local artisans who share our reverence for the land.

Today, Misk Beirut stands as a sanctuary of contemporary Lebanese dining, bridging the gap between historical heritage and modern epicurean innovation.

"An honest approach to food, focusing on the integrity of ingredients and the warmth of Lebanese tradition."
