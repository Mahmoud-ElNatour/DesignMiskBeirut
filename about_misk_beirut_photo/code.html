Our Story

Misk Beirut began as a modest kitchen in the heart of pre-war Beirut, driven by Elias Mansour's unwavering belief that the soul of Lebanese culture resides in its flavors. What started as a small gathering place for poets and painters blossomed into a sanctuary for authentic Levantine gastronomy.

Over four decades, we have remained anchored in our commitment to artisanal Levant techniques—sourcing the finest sumac from the mountains, olive oil from the groves of Koura, and hospitality from the depths of our heritage.

"An honest approach to food, focusing on the integrity of ingredients and the warmth of Lebanese tradition."
